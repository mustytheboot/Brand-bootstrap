Accent Colour Palette

Packt have chosen 9 primary accent colors shown below. These colours (and these colours only) will be used as the main base colours for all Packt products from printed books through to courses and video course cover artwork.

Each accent colour has an associated overlay colour that is fractionally darker than the accent shade. This is used as a solid background colour behind the subtitle to make the text easer to read.

Don’t use accent colors together. They’re eye-catching enough on their own and will clash if used in the same space.


The Packt brand is all about keeping it simple. Stick to this palette please! We chose these 9 lively colors carefully to contrast well with the primary brand colors, Beacon and white. We have also assigned each color to a category of content, please keep these consistent with the guidelines.
